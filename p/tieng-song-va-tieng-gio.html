Tiếng Sóng Và Tiếng Gió

The Voice of Waves and the Voice of Wind
03/05/2007 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
nổi loạn giữa đất trời
xô đẩy, huyền-hoặc sức sống
lau dạt mình và cát cuộn thân
vời-vợi chào mời bước hoang-đàng
cả một bầu trời quanh lịm tím
nắng bật vỡ huy-hoàng hoa sao thắm
trên làn da trần đẫm hơi nắng, hơi gió
mặn mùi muối ướp thân linh
ngoài xa cá quẫy
trời rộng vuột đường chim lướt gió no
cánh đo cõi cuộc rong chơi
hồn tung-toé ràn dâng không-khí
đảo cánh chao dạt vào bãi cát
rồi tung mình lướt đi xa tít-tắp
Phật xưa ngồi một chỗ
chốn rừng sâu
Tâm-thức mở tung muôn vũ-trụ
tôi nay đi trên bãi biển
hồn bật cánh dạo muôn phương

English
rebelling between Earth and Heaven
surging, bewitching the force of Life
reeds drift their bodies and sand coils itself
immensity invites the wandering step
one whole surrounding sky swoons violet
sunlight bursts glorious into crimson star-flowers
upon naked skin drenched in breath of sun, breath of wind
salt-savor embalms the spirit-body
far away fishes leap
the wide sky opens a path for birds gliding on wind replete
wings measure the realm of wandering-play
the soul splashes, brimming into air
wings turn and sway drifting toward the sand
then fling themselves to glide far beyond all distance
the Buddha of old sat in one place
within the deep forest
Consciousness opened ten thousand universes
I now walk upon the seashore
my soul takes wing journeying ten thousand ways

The Voice of Waves and the Voice of Wind
the voice of waves and the voice of wind
rebelling between Earth and Heaven
surging, bewitching the force of Life
reeds drift their bodies and sand coils itself
immensity invites the wandering step
one whole surrounding sky swoons violet
sunlight bursts glorious into crimson star-flowers
upon naked skin drenched in breath of sun, breath of wind
salt-savor embalms the spirit-body
far away fishes leap
the wide sky opens a path for birds gliding on wind replete
wings measure the realm of wandering-play
the soul splashes, brimming into air
wings turn and sway drifting toward the sand
then fling themselves to glide far beyond all distance
the Buddha of old sat in one place
within the deep forest
Consciousness opened ten thousand universes
I now walk upon the seashore
my soul takes wing journeying ten thousand ways
Translator's noteThe seated Buddha whose Consciousness opens ten thousand universes evokes enlightenment attained in forest meditation.
