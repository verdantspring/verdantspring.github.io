Bài Thơ động-Tự

Verb Poem
11/01/2007 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
tình yêu mới mọc
như những điều có thể mọc
 như mặt trăng,
 mặt trời
như cỏ hoang, cây bỗng huê đơm bông

tình yêu mới vào
như những điều có thể sang-ngang
 như bước ngang thềm cửa
 cổng nhà xay
 hạt thóc, bông lúa, mạ con gái
 lục thanh
tình yêu vừa đã chín
như những điều có thể đợi-chờ
trái vú-sữa thơm
lịm môi thơm đầu lưỡi
nụ hôn tình cuối, tình đầu
 như hoa quả thẫm, đẫm đầm-đìa
 sau làn mưa trong như
 nước mắt tình hờn
 trinh-nguyên
 tố-dông...

English
new love rises
like all things able to rise
 like the moon,
 the sun
like wild grass, a tree suddenly flowering into bloom

new love enters
like all things able to cross over
 like a step across the doorsill
 the gate of the mill-house
 grain of rice, rice blossom, maiden rice-seedling
 green sound
love has newly ripened
like all things able to await
fragrant star-apple fruit
sweetening fragrant lips upon the tongue-tip
the kiss of last love, first love
 like dark fruits, drenched and overflowing
 after a rain transparent as
 tears of a lover's anger
 virgin-pure
 storm-wind...

Verb Poem
new love rises
like all things able to rise
 like the moon,
 the sun
like wild grass, a tree suddenly flowering into bloom

new love enters
like all things able to cross over
 like a step across the doorsill
 the gate of the mill-house
 grain of rice, rice blossom, maiden rice-seedling
 green sound
love has newly ripened
like all things able to await
fragrant star-apple fruit
sweetening fragrant lips upon the tongue-tip
the kiss of last love, first love
 like dark fruits, drenched and overflowing
 after a rain transparent as
 tears of a lover's anger
 virgin-pure
 storm-wind...
Translator's noteThe star-apple is a tropical fruit familiar in Vietnam, while "maiden rice-seedling" preserves the poem's feminine image for young rice.
