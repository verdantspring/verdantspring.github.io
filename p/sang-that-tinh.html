Sáng Thất-Tình

Morning of Lost Love
09/02/2007 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
cỏ con
xanh giữa Đông
cánh đồng miền Nam
se-sẻ tụ đàn
uống sương sớm
...gió vờn da
...mây muốn quên
lòng muốn
khóc
khi tình thôi
rủ-rê
mộng-mơ...
Valentine của
chocolate và kim-cương
và mầu đỏ
hồng-nhung
...không ai
nhắc
kẻ thất-tình
đứng chơ-vơ
bên lề cuộc vui...

English
little grass
green in Winter
the Southern meadow
sparrows gather
and drink morning dew
...wind plays upon skin
...clouds wish to forget
the heart wishes
to cry
when love no longer
coaxes
Dreaming...
Valentine of
chocolate and diamonds
and the redness
of velvet-roses
...no one
remembers
the brokenhearted one
standing forlorn
at the edge of the revelry...

Morning of Lost Love
little grass
green in Winter
the Southern meadow
sparrows gather
and drink morning dew
...wind plays upon skin
...clouds wish to forget
the heart wishes
to cry
when love no longer
coaxes
Dreaming...
Valentine of
chocolate and diamonds
and the redness
of velvet-roses
...no one
remembers
the brokenhearted one
standing forlorn
at the edge of the revelry...
Translator's note“The Southern meadow” may evoke both the American South and the poet's remembered southern Việt-Nam.
