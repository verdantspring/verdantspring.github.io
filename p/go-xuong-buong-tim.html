Gõ Xuống Buồng Tim

TAPPING INTO THE HEART-CHAMBER
13/12/2006 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
Gõ xuống vào nhau những lời thư
Âm-thầm mực chảy dấu tâm-tư
Mực xanh khô đọng nên từ-ngữ
Buộc mất tâm-tình của nhau vào!
Gõ xuống vào nhau những thanh-cung
vọng vang âm-nhạc những ý-tình
Lời ca, tiếng hát rền cung-điệu
Trôi mất vào mít-mút không-thời
Còn trong trí-tưởng chút rung-động
Thả bướm tâm-hồn trên nhụy hoa.
Gõ xuống đời nhau dấu tình yêu
Biết tơ Nguyệt-Lão bền hay mỏng
Đứt-đoạn đời nhau như dây đản
Gõ xuống đời nhau những vấn-vương
Biết trang giấy ngỏ thắm hay bạc?
Loang-lấm tình nhau nét mực phai?

English
Tap into each other these words of letters
Silently ink flows, marking secret thoughts
Blue ink dries and gathers into language
Binding our heart-feelings into one another!
Tap into each other these tonal strings
music echoing the sounds of feeling-thoughts
Lyrics and singing resound through modes of music
Drifting away into endless Time-Space
Leaving within Imagination a little trembling
Releasing the soul's butterfly upon flower-pollen.
Tap upon each other's lives the mark of Love
Who knows if Nguyệt-Lão's silken thread is enduring or frail
Our lives break apart like strings of đàn
Tap upon each other's lives these lingering bonds
Who knows if the open page will glow crimson or turn pale?
Will our Love run stained in fading strokes of ink?

Tapping Into The Heart-Chamber
Tap into each other these words of letters
Silently ink flows, marking secret thoughts
Blue ink dries and gathers into language
Binding our heart-feelings into one another!
Tap into each other these tonal strings
music echoing the sounds of feeling-thoughts
Lyrics and singing resound through modes of music
Drifting away into endless Time-Space
Leaving within Imagination a little trembling
Releasing the soul's butterfly upon flower-pollen.
Tap upon each other's lives the mark of Love
Who knows if Nguyệt-Lão's silken thread is enduring or frail
Our lives break apart like strings of đàn
Tap upon each other's lives these lingering bonds
Who knows if the open page will glow crimson or turn pale?
Will our Love run stained in fading strokes of ink?
Translator's noteNguyệt-Lão is the moonlit matchmaker who binds destined lovers with an invisible red thread; đàn is a Vietnamese stringed instrument.
