Nhớ Nhớ Tình đong

Longing, Longing—Love Brimming
18/10/2007 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
nhớ ơi là nhớ, nhớ ơi!
nhớ sao đong được?
nhớ đong bằng gì?
làm sao cho đắm ao thuyền
hỏi thăm ngó nõn rễ sen
đầm bùn,
lịm vào nhung-nhớ hiển-linh
nhức tim làm cũng
bầm môi đợi chờ...
nhớ đong cho ứa ràn mi
lệ nào bỏng tận đáy hồn
ngóng tin!
nhớ ơi, nhớ hỡi, nhớ ơi,
chờ ai
mưa khóc
thay
tình thầm mong!

English
longing, O such longing, longing O!
how can longing be measured?
with what shall longing be filled?
how to drown the boat within the pond
ask after the tender lotus root
in the muddy lagoon,
faint into velvet-longing made manifest
heart aching into silence
lips bruised with waiting...
longing fills until the lashes overflow
what tears burn to the soul's very bottom
awaiting news!
longing O, O longing, longing O,
waiting for whom
rain weeps
in place of
Love's secret hope!

Longing, Longing—Love Brimming
longing, O such longing, longing O!
how can longing be measured?
with what shall longing be filled?

ask after the tender lotus root
in the muddy lagoon,
faint into velvet-longing made manifest
heart aching into silence
lips bruised with waiting...
longing fills until the lashes overflow
what tears burn to the soul's very bottom

longing O, O longing, longing O,
waiting for whom
rain weeps
in place of
Love's secret hope!
Translator's noteThe lotus rooted in mud carries the Buddhist resonance of purity and awakening born from suffering.
