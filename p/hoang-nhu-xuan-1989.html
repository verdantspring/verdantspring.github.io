HOÀNG-NHƯ-XUÂN: 1989

HOÀNG-NHƯ-XUÂN: 1989
14/05/2012 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
Một Bài Thơ 1:
 I smoked. I smoked a poem.
 A poem I smoked
 into a poem I dreamt.

 I dreamt up a poem I smoked
 that I've smoked up a poem
 that I've dreamt that I have smoked
 upon a poem I've dreamt..!!

Một Bài Thơ 2:
 Hoa hòa hóa hỏa...Bốc lửa tim tôi
 Khói lên giòng sóng
 sóng-sánh nhưng con thuyền
 xuôi ngược xuôi dòng máu đỏ
 Máu đỏ đậm xậm nguồn
 Đi về khắp châu-thân
 Hòa khói đi về khăp
 Thành bài thơ không cùng..!!


*Tập thơ chép tay với bút-hiệu là tựa đề tập thơ
English
(14/05/2012)

A Poem 1:
 I smoked. I smoked a poem.
 A poem I smoked
 into a poem I dreamt.

 I dreamt up a poem I smoked
 that I've smoked up a poem
 that I've dreamt that I have smoked
 upon a poem I've dreamt..!!

A Poem 2:
 Flower—Blending—Becoming—Fire...My heart flares ablaze
 Smoke lifts in rising waves
 swaying — yet the little boat
 downstream upstream downstream along the red blood-stream
 Red blood, deep-dark at the Source,
 Traveling throughout the whole body—
 As smoke it blends and wanders everywhere
 Becoming a poem without end..!!


*A handwritten poetry collection whose pen-name is the title of the collection
Translator's noteThe title 'Hoàng-Như-Xuân: 1989' is the poet's pen-name and the name of a handwritten collection she made that year — the poem is a meditation on that act of writing itself, so the self-referential loop in Poem 1 (smoking a poem into a dreamt poem) is intentional and complete.
