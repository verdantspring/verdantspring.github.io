Từ Văn-Lang đến Việt-Nam

From Văn-Lang to Việt-Nam
26/12/2006 · Hoàng-Ân
Tiếng Việt
Nhịp trống Tiền-Nhân! Vọng...
Tù-và vang rừng bốc dậy núi chầu
Trầm-hương ngút khói mây
Tuế-nguyệt xoay xoay:
Hai hàng Quan-Lang, Lạc-Tướng
Gọn-ghẽy-trang
Tóc đen nhánh búi trọn chặt tay
Mông-lung về từ cõi mộng
Những cửa mặt nghiêm-trang
Những môi hồng cương-nghị!
Và nữ-tướng đất Phục-Ba
Gươm cung vững-vàng
Làn thu-ba sáng quắc!
Ngày hội lễ bắt đầu
Cho con dân chiêm-ngưỡng
Hồn thiêng lặng tiếng,
"Việt-Nam Muôn Năm!"

English
The drumbeat of the Ancestors! Echoes...
The forest horn resounds, mountains rise to court
Aloeswood smoke towers into clouds
Months and years turning, turning:
Two rows of Quan-Lang, Lạc-Tướng
Neat—in ceremonial dress
Blue-black hair gathered tightly in their hands
Dimly returning from the realm of dreams
Those solemn faces
Those resolute crimson lips!
And the woman-general of Phục-Ba land
Sword and bow held steadfast
Autumn-wave eyes blazing bright!
The festival begins
For the children of the nation to behold
The Sacred Soul falls silent,
"Việt-Nam Ten Thousand Years!"

From Văn-Lang To Việt-Nam
The drumbeat of the Ancestors! Echoes...
The forest horn resounds, mountains rise to court
Aloeswood smoke towers into clouds
Months and years turning, turning:
Two rows of Quan-Lang, Lạc-Tướng
Neat—in ceremonial dress
Blue-black hair gathered tightly in their hands
Dimly returning from the realm of dreams
Those solemn faces
Those resolute crimson lips!
And the woman-general of Phục-Ba land
Sword and bow held steadfast
Autumn-wave eyes blazing bright!
The festival begins
For the children of the nation to behold
The Sacred Soul falls silent,
"Việt-Nam Ten Thousand Years!"
Translator's noteVăn-Lang is the legendary first Vietnamese kingdom; Quan-Lang and Lạc-Tướng were titles of its nobility and generals.
